Casa Bevk

Casa Bevk is a private family hub designed to simplify everyday life. It combines shared to-do lists, shopping lists, personal notes, a family calendar, and household finances in one secure and intuitive place.

Built for two users with a focus on simplicity and collaboration, Casa Bevk helps manage daily tasks, track expenses, organize budgets, and keep important information easily accessible from any device.

Features:
Shared To-Do Lists
Shopping Lists
Personal and Shared Notes
Family Calendar and Reminders
Expense Tracking
Budget Management
Recurring Payments and Subscriptions
Dashboard with Daily Overview
Secure Authentication
Real-time Synchronization

Casa Bevk is not another social app. It is a private digital home for organizing what matters most.
